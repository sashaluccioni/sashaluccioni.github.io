About me:

I'm a researcher in ethical and sustainable artificial intelligence at HuggingFace, as well as a Founding Member of Climate Change AI and a Board Member of Women in Machine Learning.

My work focuses on having a better understanding of the societal and environmental impacts of AI models, datasets and systems.

Recent news:

  • The NeurIPS Code of Ethics, which I co-led, is out!
  • I was a co-lead organizer of the Tackling Climate Change with Machine Learning workshop, which took place in Kigali Rwanda.
  • I joined the WiML Board of Directors in Spring 2022.
